# Community Code of Conduct

MascotRender is built for expressive characters representing many people,
communities, and applications. Contributors and maintainers are expected to
make participation respectful and constructive.

Acceptable behavior includes thoughtful technical disagreement, specific and
actionable review, respect for names and identities, acknowledgment of limits
in one's cultural knowledge, and willingness to correct harmful or inaccurate
representation.

Harassment, personal attacks, sexualized attention, threats, deliberate
misgendering, discriminatory stereotypes, doxxing, and sustained disruption are
not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and communication that violate
these expectations and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation.
Reports should use a private maintainer or GitHub reporting channel where
possible. Maintainers handling a report should protect privacy, avoid conflicts
of interest, document the disposition, and respond proportionately.
